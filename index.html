About Bhavsar Udyog Application

Bhavsar Udyog is a mobile and web application built to empower the Bhavsar community by connecting entrepreneurs, startups, and organizations on one trusted platform. It serves as a business directory, networking hub, and growth ecosystem, helping members showcase their work, reach customers, and build meaningful collaborations.

Key Features

User Profiles

Create a verified business profile with owner details, contact info, and brochures.

Business Directory

Discover businesses across categories, states, and cities in one platform.

Smart Search

Find businesses quickly using filters for location, type of business, or service.

Google Maps

Locate shops and offices easily with integrated Google Map links.

Notifications

Stay updated with community news, approvals, and opportunities.

Privacy & Security

Your data is protected, and users can request account deletion anytime.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To build a transparent and supportive ecosystem where Bhavsar entrepreneurs and organizations grow together with trust and collaboration.

Vision: To be the leading platform that connects tradition with modern entrepreneurship, helping Bhavsar businesses expand globally while staying rooted in community values.
